Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is the best device to view the platform on?
    The ILF platform has been specially designed for a mobile first approach, however the platform is responsive so will be viewable on tablets and desktops.
  2. How do you log in to the platform?
    To login, you will have to enter either your email address or mobile number. This will generate a OTP that will be sent to you.
  3. How do I logout of the app?
    To logout, simple click the 3 lines on the top left corner and select ‘LOGOUT’
  4. Will I have to use the same process to login each time?
    Once you log out, you will need to follow the same steps to login and a new OTP will be sent to you
  5. How do I contact the event team to update my email or mobile number?
    To contact the event team, click the 3 lines on the top left hand corner and select ‘CONTACT’. From here, you can send through any queries and the team will get in touch.
  6. How to bookmark or save the app for easy access?
    It is a good idea to bookmark the site and if viewing on a mobile device, save the app to the home screen for easy access.

    SAVE THE APP TO YOUR HOME SCREEN:

    Apple: When in the safari app touch, select the Share icon, then tap the 'Add to Home Screen' option.
    Please note: Currently this is only supported by the Safari browser in iOS - other browsers are not supported.

    Android: Most phones will detect the application and automatically prompt users to save it to their home screen when opening the App for the first time. If the prompt is not seen, or it has been dismissed, to Add to Home Screen, simply select the Add App to Home Screen option found in the browser menu.

    BOOKMARK ON YOUR PHONE:

    Apple: When in the safari app touch and hold the book icon then tap 'Add Bookmark'

    Android: When in the browser, touch the Star icon when viewing the page. The Star icon may be found at the top of the screen, or you may find it on the browser menu. Once selected, tap 'Add Bookmark'

  7. Where do I go to edit my profile?
    To edit your profile, click the person icon on the top right hand side of the screen and select ‘Profile” - make sure to click save at the bottom of the screen for any changes that you make.
  8. How do I upload a picture to my profile?
    To upload your profile picture, simply tap the white circle ‘Tap here to select a photo from your gallery’. From here, you can choose to either select a photo from your library or take a live photo.
  9. How do I edit my privacy settings?
    To edit your privacy settings, click on the person icon on the top right hand corner and select ‘Privacy’. From here you can control and manage the visibility of your information
  10. What is Networking?
    The networking area of the platform allows community members to connect with other leaders, exchange ideas, and unearth new strategies to achieve drive transformation within their organisation.
  11. How to be discoverable?
    To utilise the networking functionality, community members must be discoverable. To be discoverable, they simply need to navigate toward the Profile Privacy page and Enable 'Discoverable in Networking'
  12. How to get the most out of the Networking functionality?
    • Set up profile - Click on your name in the event menu to begin
    • Find others - Use the Find tab within the Networking block to discover new connections
    • Approve requests - Find people who want to connect with you and approve them in Requests.
    • Chat with others - Click the chat icon to start a person-to-person chat. Find it in the bottom right-hand corner.
    • View your connections - View all the connections you have made in the Connections tab.
  13. How do I send someone a message on the app?
    You can message someone on the app by clicking on the message icon on the bottom right hand corner. Alternatively, you can click on the networking tab and scroll down to ‘List’ which will show you all the people on the network, and click the message icon next to their name.
  14. How to find upcoming events?

  15. How to RSVP to an event?
    To RSVP to an event, click on ‘RSVP FOR THIS EVENT’ which can be found under the event summary.
  16. How do I open an insight in a new tab?
    To open an insight in a new tab, simply hold down on the insight image and select ‘Open in New Tab’.
